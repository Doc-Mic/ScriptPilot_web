## Before writing

Define the viewer, the promise, and the reason the video should exist today.
That gives the script a job before any lines are drafted.

## During drafting

Start with the hook, then build toward a payoff. Keep each section focused on
one clear thought so the viewer always knows why they should keep watching.

## After drafting

Read the script aloud, remove repeated points, and tighten anything that slows
the first minute.
