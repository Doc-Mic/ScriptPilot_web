## Start with the signal

Trend research is about finding what people are already starting to care about,
then shaping that signal into a topic your channel can own.

## Keep the workflow simple

Use a clear topic, a specific audience, and a practical angle. A useful trend is
not just popular. It should also be something your viewers would click, watch,
and remember.
